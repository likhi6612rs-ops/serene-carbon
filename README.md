# 🌿 Serene: Your Personal Carbon Footprint Companion

[![License: Proprietary](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-Proprietary-red.svg)](https://opensource.org/license/proprietary)

## 📝 Description
**Serene** is a modern, intuitive web application designed to empower individuals to take control of their environmental impact. By simplifying the way users track, visualize, and analyze their daily habits, Serene transforms complex environmental data into actionable insights, helping users build a more sustainable lifestyle with ease and clarity.

## 🚀 Key Features
* **Intuitive Carbon Tracking:** Effortlessly log daily activities—from transportation choices to energy consumption—using a clean, user-friendly interface.
* **Real-Time Visualization:** View your environmental impact through dynamic, easy-to-understand charts and graphs that highlight your progress over time.
* **Personalized Sustainability Insights:** Receive contextual, data-driven tips and behavioral nudges designed to help you reduce your footprint based on your specific lifestyle.
* **Goal-Oriented Dashboard:** Set, track, and achieve personal carbon reduction targets to stay motivated and witness the tangible result of your positive actions.
* **Responsive & Lightweight:** Built with a focus on accessibility and performance, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Frontend:** React.js, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
* **Backend:** Firebase (Authentication and Database)
* **Deployment:** Vercel

## 💡 The Serene Mission
Sustainability shouldn't be complicated. Serene was built on the belief that small, consistent changes in our daily choices can lead to a significant collective impact. Our goal is to make eco-conscious living rewarding, transparent, and accessible to everyone.
